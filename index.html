My Story

My fascination with web development started when my boss asked me to build an ecommerce site for the company. As I did not have any proper skillsets at that time, I used Shopify in order to build one. As I was the sole person tasked to manage the site, I had to pick up a variety of HTMl/CSS and JavaScript skills in order to do minor edits to the site. My interest grew from there with me taking up remote courses from Codecademy and eventually taking the plunge into a full time software engineering immersive course at General Assembly.
