Q-Tea

A Queue-ing app for consumers to send their orders to the shop to prepare ahead of time.

Q-Tea

Skip the hassle of 15 min queue times!

During my time in GA, lunch break was only an hour long. Walking and queueing time could take up to 20-30 mins depending on the lunch crowd of the day, which often mean that we had less time than we like to have our lunch. Q-tea was borne out of the desire to address the problem of long queue times by sending your orders to the shop ahead of time so that it will be prepared by the time you walk over.

Built using the MVC framework with HTML/CSS and JavaScript, it uses posgresSQL to store the data in its backend. The site itself has 2 interfaces, 1 for the consumer to place their orders, and the other for the admin to track current orders. In addition, it also makes use of Twilo api to send sms confirmations once orders are placed.
